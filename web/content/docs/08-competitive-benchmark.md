# 08 — Competitive Benchmark & Market Structure

> Added in the consulting layer. Answers "do we need better market comparison?" — yes, and
> here it is, with the single most important structural signal: **the market is consolidating.**

## The headline signal: consolidation
**In June 2025, Mintel acquired Black Swan Data.** Ai Palette's strongest *pure-play* predictive
competitor is now absorbed into a slow, expensive incumbent. Strategic reading:

- The "real-time social prediction" capability is being rolled into legacy research subscriptions.
- That **vacates the nimble, end-to-end, NPD-native position** — exactly where Ai Palette sits.
- It creates **urgency**: incumbents are buying their way into AI prediction. Ai Palette must
  lock in differentiated position (APAC depth + concept-to-screen workflow) before the window closes.

## Positioning 2×2 (the white space)
Axes: **breadth of workflow** (single insight ↔ end-to-end discover→concept→screen) ×
**geographic/localization depth** (Western-centric ↔ multi-market APAC+).

```
 end-to-end  │                         │  ◆ Ai Palette
 workflow    │   Tastewise ○           │   (own this corner)
 ────────────┼─────────────────────────┼──────────────────────
 single      │   Spate ○  Datassential │   Mintel ○ (broad geo,
 insight     │   (US/vertical)    ○    │   not NPD-native)
             │                         │   Black Swan → Mintel
             └─────────────────────────┴──────────────────────
              Western-centric            multi-market / APAC depth
```

**White space = upper-right:** end-to-end NPD workflow **with** genuine multi-market/APAC
localization depth. No incumbent owns it post-consolidation. This is the strategic anchor for
the whole MarketPulse plan.
